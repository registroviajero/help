# Team and roles

RegistroViajero lets you work as a team with different access levels.

## Available roles

| Role | Accommodations | Reservations | Team | Settings | Billing | Audit |
|------|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|
| **Owner** | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ |
| **Administrator** | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ | — | ✓ |
| **Member** | — | ✓ | — | — | — | ✓ |

- **Owner** — full access, including billing and team management.
- **Administrator** — manages accommodations, reservations, team, and settings (no billing).
- **Member** — manages reservations and views the audit log.

## Invite members

1. Go to **Settings → Team**.
2. Click **Invite member**.
3. Enter the email and pick a role.
4. The invitee will receive an email with a link to create their account.

::: info
Invitations expire after 7 days if not accepted. You can resend or cancel an invitation at any time.
:::

## Manage the team

From the Team section you can:

- Change a member's role
- Remove a member from the team
- See pending invitations

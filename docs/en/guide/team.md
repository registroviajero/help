# Team and roles

RegistroViajero lets you work as a team with different access levels.

::: tip Unlimited team
**Team members are unlimited** — you don't pay per person. The invoice is calculated only on the number of active accommodations. Adding teammates is free.
:::

## Available roles

| Role | Accommodations | Reservations | SES credentials | Team | Settings | Billing | Audit |
|------|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|
| **Owner** | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ |
| **Administrator** | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ | — | ✓ |
| **Member** | — | ✓ | — | — | — | — | ✓ |

- **Owner** — full access, including billing and team management. There is exactly one Owner per agency.
- **Administrator** — manages accommodations, reservations, SES credentials, team, and settings. Cannot access billing.
- **Member** — manages reservations and views the activity log. Cannot edit accommodations or credentials.

## Invite members

1. Go to **Settings → Team**.
2. Click **Invite member**.
3. Enter the email and pick a role.
4. The invitee will receive an email with a link to create their account.

::: info
Invitations expire after 7 days if not accepted. You can resend or cancel an invitation at any time.
:::

## Manage the team

From the Team section you can:

- Change a member's role.
- Remove a member from the team.
- See pending invitations and resend or cancel them.

## Transfer ownership

If the current Owner leaves the company, open a support request: we will validate the change and transfer the role. The transfer is logged in the [activity log](/en/guide/activity-log).

# Billing

## Available plans

| Plan | Price | Includes |
|------|-------|----------|
| **Trial** | €0 (15 days) | Full access, Ministry submission, no credit card |
| **Pro** | €5 per accommodation/month | No minimum, automatic proration when activating/deactivating, unlimited guests, unlimited team, CSV/PDF export, priority support |

Billing is monthly and processed through [Polar](https://polar.sh). RegistroViajero does not store credit card data.

::: info How the invoice is calculated
Each **active accommodation** adds €5/month. Team size does not affect billing — adding teammates is free. When you activate or deactivate an accommodation, Polar prorates the amount automatically on the next invoice.
:::

## Free trial

When you sign up, you get a **15-day free trial** with full access, including Ministry submission. No credit card required.

When the trial ends without subscribing to Pro:

- Ministry submission is **blocked**.
- Data remains accessible in read-only mode.
- Check-in links **keep working** with no restrictions.

To resume submission, subscribe to Pro from **Settings → Billing** (Owner only).

## Subscription states

| State | Can submit to Ministry? | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Trialing** | Yes | Trial period active |
| **Active** | Yes | Pro subscription up to date |
| **Cancelled** | Yes, until the end of the paid period | You keep full access until that date |
| **Past due** | No | We could not charge you — update your payment method |
| **No subscription** | No | Trial expired or cancellation lapsed |

## Manage your subscription

Billing is available at **Settings → Billing** (only accessible to the **Owner** role).

From there you can:

- **Activate or deactivate accommodations** — each change re-bills accordingly.
- See your payment history and upcoming invoices.
- Update your payment method through the Polar portal.
- Cancel your subscription.

## Cancellation

- You can cancel at any time.
- You keep full access (including Ministry submission) until the end of the paid period.
- Once the paid period ends, the account becomes read-only: Ministry submission is blocked, but your data and check-in links remain available.
- No refunds are issued for partial periods.

## Failed payments

If Polar cannot charge you (expired card, insufficient funds, etc.), the subscription moves to **Past due** and Ministry submission is blocked until you update your payment method.

Guests can still fill in their data without issue; only the Ministry submission is paused.

## Discount codes

If you have a discount code, you apply it on the Polar checkout during sign-up. Codes are not stackable.

::: tip Important
Guest check-in is **never blocked** by subscription status. Even with the plan expired or cancelled, guests can still complete their data.
:::

# Billing

## Available plans

| Plan | Price | Includes |
|------|-------|----------|
| **Trial** | €0 (15 days) | Full access, unlimited guests, Ministry submission |
| **Pro** | €5 per accommodation/month | Only pay for active accommodations, no minimum. Unlimited guests, CSV/PDF export, priority support |

Billing is monthly. When you activate or deactivate accommodations, the amount is prorated automatically on the next invoice.

## Free trial

When you sign up, you get a **15-day free trial** with full access. No credit card required. After the trial, you'll need to subscribe to the Pro plan to keep submitting reports to the Ministry.

## Manage your subscription

Billing is available at **Settings → Billing** (only accessible to the **Owner** role).

From there you can:

- Activate or deactivate accommodations
- See your payment history and upcoming invoices
- Update your payment method
- Cancel your subscription

Payments are processed through [Polar](https://polar.sh). RegistroViajero does not store credit card data.

## Cancellation

- You can cancel at any time.
- Access is retained until the end of the paid period.
- After cancellation, you won't be able to submit new reports to the Ministry, but existing guest check-in links continue to work.
- No refunds are issued for partial periods.

## Discount codes

If you have a discount code, you can apply it during the subscription flow.

::: tip Important
Guest check-in is **never blocked** by subscription status. Even if your plan lapses, guests can still complete their data through the check-in links.
:::

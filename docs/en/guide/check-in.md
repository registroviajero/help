# Check-in link

Each guest receives a **unique link** to complete their registration. They don't need to sign up, install anything, or download anything — they simply open the link on their phone or browser.

## How it works

1. From the reservation, click **Add guest**.
2. A unique link is generated for each guest.
3. Share the link via WhatsApp, email, or any other channel.
4. You can also share the **QR code** generated for each link (useful for in-person sharing).

## The guest form

The guest fills in their data in 4 steps:

1. **Identification** — nationality, document type, document number
2. **Personal details** — name, surnames, sex, date of birth
3. **Address and contact** — address, phone, email
4. **Document photos** — front and back photo of the identity document

They then sign a **declaration of accuracy** and confirm submission.

::: info
Data is saved automatically after each step. If the guest closes the browser, they can resume later by reopening the same link.
:::

## Document rules

- **Spanish citizens:** DNI or passport
- **Non-Spanish:** passport, NIE, EU registration certificate, foreign national ID, or travel document
- **DNI, NIE, and EU certificate** require the **support number** (printed on the back of the card)
- **DNI / NIE** require a second surname
- **Passport, foreign national ID, and travel document** only require a photo of the front

## Minors

- **Under 14:** exempt from registration under Royal Decree 933/2021.
- **14 to 17:** must complete the form and declare their kinship with an adult on the same reservation.

## Available languages

The check-in form is available in 9 languages: Spanish, English, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Galician, Basque, and Catalan.

## Group link

You can generate a **group link** that bundles the individual links of every guest on a reservation into a single page. Useful when you want to send just one message to the lead guest.

# Create your account

## Sign up

1. Go to [app.registroviajero.com](https://app.registroviajero.com) and click **Create account**.
2. Enter your name, email, and password.
3. You'll receive a verification email — click the link to activate your account.
4. Fill in your agency details: trading name and CIF/NIF.

## Free trial

When you sign up, you get a **15-day free trial** with full access. No credit card required.

After the trial, you'll need to subscribe to the [Pro plan](/en/guide/billing) to keep submitting to the Ministry. Guest check-in links are never blocked by subscription status.

## Account security

- Passwords are hashed with argon2id.
- You can change your password from your profile at any time.
- If you forget your password, use the **Forgot your password?** option on the sign-in screen.
- Changing your password invalidates all active sessions automatically.

## Delete your account

You can delete your account from **Settings → Profile → Danger zone**. You'll have to type `DELETE` to confirm. Personal data is anonymized; audit records are retained for legal compliance.

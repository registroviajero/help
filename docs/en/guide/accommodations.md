# Add accommodations

## Create an accommodation

1. Go to **Accommodations** in the side menu.
2. Click **New accommodation**.
3. Fill in the details:
   - **Name** of the accommodation
   - **Full address** (street, number, city, province, postcode)
   - **Type** of accommodation (apartment, hotel, rural house, guesthouse, etc.)
   - **Establishment code** issued by the Ministry of the Interior
   - **SES credentials** — pick the [credentials](/en/guide/ses-credentials) that apply to this accommodation

::: info
Provinces in Catalonia and the Basque Country are not listed in the selector, since these regions use their own systems (Mossos d'Esquadra and Ertzaintza).
:::

## Multiple accommodations

You can manage all your accommodations from a single agency account. Each accommodation has its own establishment code and can use different SES credentials.

Billing is calculated on **active** accommodations:

- **Trial:** 15 days of full access, no credit card.
- **Pro plan:** €5 per accommodation per month, no minimum. You only pay for active accommodations; deactivated ones don't count.

## Edit or deactivate

You can edit an accommodation's details at any time from the modal form. If an accommodation is no longer in use, you can deactivate it — existing reservations remain accessible, but you won't be able to create new ones for it.

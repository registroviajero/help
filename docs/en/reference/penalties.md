# Penalties under RD 933/2021

**Royal Decree 933/2021** establishes the duty to submit guest reports, but it **does not set the fines** directly. The applicable penalty regime is **Organic Law 4/2015, of 30 March, on the protection of public safety**.

::: info
This page is an informational reference. The final classification and amount of a penalty is determined by the competent authority in each case.
:::

## Primary classification

Article **37.3** of LO 4/2015 classifies as a **minor offense**:

> *"Omission or inaccuracy of the submission to the Registry of the data referred to in the public-safety regulations in relation to travellers and persons staying at accommodation establishments."*

Failure to submit — or submitting with errors — the guest report falls, by default, under this provision.

## Amounts by severity

| Severity | Minimum | Maximum |
|----------|--------:|--------:|
| **Minor** | €100 | €600 |
| **Serious** | €601 | €30,000 |
| **Very serious** | €30,001 | €600,000 |

Within each tier there are grades (minimum, medium, maximum) that the authority applies by weighing intent, damage caused, financial capacity, and repeat offenses.

## Escalation to serious or very serious

The classification can be elevated when aggravating circumstances concur:

- **Repeat** non-compliance not corrected.
- **Significant volume** of omitted submissions.
- **Deliberate concealment** or alteration of data.
- **Obstruction** of the inspection authority.

## Applicable reductions

The general administrative penalty procedure allows:

- **20% reduction** for voluntary acknowledgement of responsibility.
- **20% reduction** for early payment.

Both reductions can be combined in some cases.

## Competent bodies

| Territory | Authority |
|-----------|-----------|
| Most Spanish regions | Ministry of the Interior (DGP / Guardia Civil) |
| Catalonia | Competent department + Mossos d'Esquadra |
| Basque Country | Department of Security + Ertzaintza |

## Scenarios that end in a penalty

- OTA reservations (Booking, Airbnb) without a submitted report, detected via data cross-checks.
- **Late submissions** (more than 24 hours after the start of the stay).
- **Incomplete or incorrect data** (missing second surname, support number, kinship).
- **Cancellations** not communicated after the report was submitted.
- Properties **not registered** as an establishment in SES.HOSPEDAJES.

## Operational mitigation

- [SES credentials](/en/guide/ses-credentials) configured and verified.
- [iCal import](/en/guide/ical) active so OTA reservations aren't missed.
- [Validate and submit](/en/guide/submit) within the 24-hour window from check-in.
- Immutable audit log available as evidence of due diligence.
